# VoltDeck

**NEC 2026 calculator and reference for working electricians.**

VoltDeck is a code-reference and calculation tool built for licensed electrical professionals working in the field. It is currently an early release.

## What's in it

**9 calculators** — Voltage Drop · Conduit Fill · Box Fill · Wire Ampacity · Motor Branch Circuit · Three-Phase / Single-Phase · Grounding & Bonding · OCPD (Breaker) Sizing · Dwelling-Unit Service Load.

**Reference library** — Ampacity (310.16) · Grounding electrode conductor (250.66) · Equipment grounding conductor (250.122) · Conductor properties (Ch. 9 Table 8) · Raceway fill (Ch. 9 Tables 1, 4, 5) · Color codes · Working clearances (110.26) · Standard OCPD ratings · Box volume + conductor allowance.

**Jobs** — group calculations by site or project. Saved results are an immutable audit trail. Optional iCloud sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

**NEC editions** — NEC 2026 at launch. Support for older editions (2017, 2020, 2023) may be added later as free updates if there is enough user demand and the citations can be verified against the NEC text. No older edition is guaranteed.

## Designed for the field

- Big tap targets, glove-friendly
- High contrast, readable in direct sunlight
- Works fully offline — no signal, no problem
- No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no data collection

## Pricing

- **Free** — 3 calculators (Voltage Drop, Wire Ampacity, OCPD), full reference library, 3 saved jobs
- **Pro** — all 9 calculators, unlimited jobs, every NEC edition supported in v1.x
  - **$39.99** lifetime (one-time)
  - **$14.99 / year** (subscription)

## Important

VoltDeck is an early release. Calculations have not been independently audited or certified. Verify every result against another calculator, hand calculation, or the NEC text before relying on it. Obtain AHJ approval for any installation that depends on a VoltDeck calculation. See the full [disclaimer](/disclaimer/).
